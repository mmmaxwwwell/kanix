Changed `api/src/db/queries/evidence.ts:288` from `textContent: r.textContent ? JSON.parse(r.textContent) : null` to `textContent: r.textContent`. Evidence records in the DB store plain strings (confirmed by psql query), not JSON. `JSON.parse` was throwing `SyntaxError` and propagating as a 500. The bundle is for human review so returning raw text is correct and lower risk than adding a try/catch that silently swallows parse errors.
